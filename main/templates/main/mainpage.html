This is a wiki site to publish political and other initiatives and petitions throughout the world. It supports all world languages.

This site has unique features:

  • Everybody can edit a petition.
  • Community-based moderation, only other users like you can decide to remove an initiative.
  • A petition can be translated into other languages. We support at least 8115 languages.
  • Up and down votes for every petition.
  • (TBD) If a version (that is an edition) of a petition is marked spam, the spam votes will be removed.
  • (Currently non-functional) Petitions can be world-wide or bound to a country, region, city, or a district.
